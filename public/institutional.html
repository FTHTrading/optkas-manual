Platform Summary

OPTKAS is an institutional-grade settlement and control infrastructure operating a structured credit program. The platform issues, settles, and governs bond-like instruments using XRPL and Stellar as native settlement rails — under OPTKAS1-MAIN SPV (Wyoming, USA) with independent custody, multisig governance, and dual-chain evidence anchoring. No single entity controls more than one layer.
