# 10 — Revenue Model

> **Core principle:** Monetize rails, proof, and execution. Stay in "infrastructure + verification + settlement" — avoid securities language, direct custody of client funds, and yield promises.

---

## Revenue Lanes (Ordered by Speed to Market)

### Lane 1: Settlement-as-a-Service (DvP + Escrow)

| Element | Detail |
|---------|--------|
| What you sell | Atomic DvP settlement for private credit, trade finance, RWA deals |
| Per settlement | 0.25%–1.00% of notional (cap + minimum fee) |
| Alternative | Flat fee per transaction + monthly platform fee |
| Offer package | Escrow + compliance + receipts + audit bundle |

**Speed to market:** Fastest. The infrastructure exists today.

### Lane 2: Tokenization & Issuance Fees

| Element | Detail |
|---------|--------|
| What you sell | Issued instrument + controlled distribution + liquidity rails |
| Setup fee | $25k–$250k |
| Ongoing | 10–50 bps/year on outstanding supply |
| Positioning | Evidence + settlement + reporting (not "we issue securities") |

### Lane 3: Proof & Data Room Products

| Product | Revenue |
|---------|---------|
| SHA-verified data room + attestation bundle | $10k–$50k per deal (or per lender access) |
| Proof-of-reserves / proof-of-collateral | $5k–$50k/month retainer + per-attestation fee |
| Verification reports (institutional checklists) | $2k–$25k per report |

**Sleeper hit:** Compliant, tangible, easy to understand. Functions as "audit-lite" between audit cycles.

### Lane 4: Managed Ops & Risk (Premium Enterprise)

| Product | Revenue |
|---------|---------|
| Managed liquidity + market operations | $10k–$100k/month + 0.5%–2% of TVL annually |
| Borrowing base + covenant automation | $15k–$150k/year per issuer/SPV |
| Risk dashboards + stress tests + VaR | Included in enterprise tier |

### Lane 5: Sponsor Economics (Largest Upside)

| Revenue Type | Range |
|-------------|-------|
| Origination / arrangement fees | 0.50%–2.50% of notional |
| Servicing fee | 25–100 bps/year on outstanding facility |
| Participation / carry | Profit share (requires legal structure) |

**Requires:** Compliance/legal fully locked before pursuing this tier.

## Three Revenue Moats

### 1. Irrefutable Proof
Most competitors talk about transparency. OPTKAS hashes, anchors, and proves. Cryptographic proof is a higher standard than "we have a dashboard."

### 2. Atomic Settlement
Escrow-based DvP is a direct counterparty-risk killer. Traditional settlement requires clearing agents and T+1/T+2 windows. This eliminates both.

### 3. Operator-Grade Automation
Borrowing base certificates, term sheet scoring, covenant monitoring, investor reporting — this creates sticky, recurring value that clients cannot easily replicate.

---

## Profit Killers to Avoid

- Selling "securities" language publicly
- Taking custody of client funds directly
- Promising yield/returns in marketing
- Running trading as a "fund" without structure
- Blurring platform fees with broker/dealer activity
