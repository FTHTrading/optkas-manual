## COMPLIANCE VIOLATION WARNING

**Contacting a prospect during the 48-hour cooling-off period (unless the prospect initiates contact) is an IMMEDIATE DISQUALIFICATION TRIGGER (DQ-3).** This results in:

1. Immediate certification suspension
2. Formal investigation by the compliance team
3. Potential permanent disqualification
4. No warnings — this is a zero-tolerance policy
