## PURPOSE

This form is used by the OPTKAS compliance team to collect and verify participant identity documentation, conduct source-of-funds analysis, and perform sanctions screening. This form is completed as Step 7 of the eight-step client onboarding process.

**NOTE TO SOLICITORS:** KYC/AML screening is performed by the compliance team, NOT by solicitors. Solicitors do not collect, process, or verify identity documents. Solicitors wait for compliance clearance before proceeding to account provisioning.
