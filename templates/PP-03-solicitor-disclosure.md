The person identified above is a **Platform Solicitor** for OPTKAS1-MAIN SPV. The Solicitor:

- **IS** authorized to present verified facts about the OPTKAS platform
- **IS** authorized to answer technical questions using approved materials
- **IS** authorized to facilitate the onboarding process for qualified participants
