## Eight-Step Onboarding Document Flow

```
Step 1: Initial Contact & Qualification ──── AI-001, SIL-001
Step 2: Proof Pack Delivery ──────────────── PP-01 through PP-08, OB-001
Step 3: 48-Hour Cooling-Off ──────────────── CO-001
Step 4: Risk Disclosure Walk-Through ─────── PP-02
Step 5: Questions & Evidence ─────────────── SIL-001
Step 6: Agreement Execution ──────────────── PP-03 [FIRST], PP-02, PP-01, PP-08, AI-001
Step 7: Compliance Verification ──────────── KYC-001
Step 8: Account Provisioning ─────────────── OB-001 Completion
```

---

## Mandatory Signing Order (Step 6)

1. **PP-03** — Solicitor Disclosure Form (ALWAYS FIRST)
2. **PP-02** — Risk Disclosure Acknowledgment
3. **PP-01** — Participation Agreement
4. **PP-08** — Fee Schedule Acknowledgment
5. **AI-001** — Accredited Investor Certification
6. **CO-001** — Cooling-Off Confirmation

> **ALL documents must be signed BEFORE any funds are collected.**
